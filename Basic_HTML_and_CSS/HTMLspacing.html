Wolves

The wolf, also known as the gray wolf or grey wolf,
is a large canine native to Eurasia and North America.
More than thirty subspecies of Canis lupus have been recognized,
and gray wolves, as popularly understood, comprise non-domestic/feral subspecies.
The wolf is the largest extant member of the family Canidae.

Wolves are native to:  Eurasia and North America
